Legal Aspects of Finance SCDL Solved Paper - 55

This solved paper of SCDL Legal Aspects of Finance of SCDL Pune has been contributed by XYZ

 Multiple Choice Multiple Answer
  Question  
How can you compute available surplus under section 5?
  Answer  
Gross profit less deductions , Direct tax calculated in accordance to provisions of section 7 in respect to amount equal to gross profits of the employer for immediately preceding accounting year
 True/False
  Question  
Every company has to appoint an auditor to audit the accounts of the company and has to fix remuneration to be paid to the auditor.
  Answer  
TRUE
 Multiple Choice Single Answer
  Question  
FEMA Norms are compatible with :-
  Answer  
FERA
 Select The Blank
  Question  
The definition of ECB in India is partly in alignment with ________ Act.
  Answer  
FERA
 Select The Blank
  Question  
Articles of Association are rules for ________.
  Answer  
Internal management
 Multiple Choice Multiple Answer
  Question  
Section 24 provides bar to entertain suits by the court for recovery of wages in so far as the sum claimed falls within which of the following categories :-
  Answer  
For which any application claim has already been presented , Direction of claim already given' , Amount adjudged is not due to penalty
 Multiple Choice Single Answer
  Question  
What is the minimum salary under which the persons are covered in this act?
  Answer  
Less than Rs 1600
 Select The Blank
  Question  
________ of the company has a right to take inspection of the books of account.
  Answer  
Any Director
 True/False
  Question  
The minimum wages act came in force on 15 march 1968
  Answer  
FALSE
 Select The Blank
  Question  
The recommendations of audit committee relating to financial management shall be ________.
  Answer  
Binding
 Multiple Choice Multiple Answer
  Question  
Who are governed by the payment of wages act?
  Answer  
Employed persons , Salary less than 1600 per month
